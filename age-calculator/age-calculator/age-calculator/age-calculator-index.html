Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool secure?

Absolutely. Your images are never uploaded to our servers. All compression is performed locally in your browser, ensuring your privacy is 100% protected.

What image formats are supported?

Our tool works with the most common web formats: JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. The output will be in JPEG format to ensure the best compression ratios.

Is there a file size limit?

There are no hard limits enforced by our tool. However, performance may vary depending on your device's processing power for extremely large images (e.g., > 20MB).
