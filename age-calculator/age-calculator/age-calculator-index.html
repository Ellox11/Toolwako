Common Questions

How is the age calculated?

Our calculator determines the time difference between your date of birth and the current date. It accurately accounts for leap years and the varying number of days in each month to give you a precise result in years, months, and days.

Is this age calculator accurate?

Yes, the algorithm is designed to be highly accurate for calculating chronological age. It provides the same results you would get from a manual calculation that properly handles calendar complexities.
